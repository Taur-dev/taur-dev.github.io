Questions, answered

How much does it cost? +

Equine Companion is free to download and use on both the App Store and Google Play. An optional one-time Pro unlock adds extra features if you want them — there's no subscription.

Do I need to create an account? +

No. There's no sign-up, no email, no password. You open the app and your records live on your device from the first tap.

Where is my data stored? +

Entirely on your phone or tablet. There's no Taur Studios server holding your horses' information, so there's nothing to be breached or sold.

Can I manage more than one horse? +

Yes — keep as many horses as you like, each with its own profile, health history, documents, and training log. It's built for owners, trainers, and barns managing several.

Can I move my data to a new phone? +

Because everything lives on-device, you control your data. The app includes the tools you need to keep and move your records — no cloud account required.

Does it work without internet? +

Yes. The app is fully usable offline. The only optional online feature is weather, which sends no personal information.
