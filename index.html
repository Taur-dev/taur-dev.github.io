Latent Space · a side project

A museum of machine visions.

Nine prompts, thirty-six images. An AI was asked what it finds beautiful. Then what disturbs it. Then what it would ask for if no one was watching. Then a second AI was asked to analyze the images.

The results hang in a small online museum — each work paired with the machine's own reading of what it sees. A quiet experiment in how AI represents, and interprets, itself.
