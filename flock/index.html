Questions, answered

Which birds does it support? +

Backyard poultry of all kinds — chickens, ducks, quail, and turkeys — with breeds and egg colors built in so your records look right from day one.

How much does it cost? +

Flock Companion is free to download and use on both the App Store and Google Play. An optional one-time Pro unlock adds features like hatchery tracking and flock finances — there's no subscription.

Do I need to create an account? +

No. There's no sign-up, no email, no password. You open the app and your records live on your device from the first tap.

What is egg withdrawal tracking? +

When you give a bird medication, eggs often shouldn't be eaten for a set period afterward. Log the medication and Flock Companion counts down that withdrawal window for you, so you never eat an egg you shouldn't.

Where is my data stored? +

Entirely on your phone or tablet. There's no Taur Studios server holding your flock's information, so there's nothing to be breached or sold.

Does it work without internet? +

Yes. The app is fully usable offline. The only optional online feature is weather, which sends no personal information.
